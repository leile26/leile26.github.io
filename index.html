About Me

I am a senior machine learning engineer at LinkedIn, Sunnyvale. Before joining LinkedIn, I was an applied scientist at Amazon, Seattle. Before joining Amazon, I obtained my PhD degree in Computer Science, from School of Informatics, Computing and Engineering, Indiana University Bloomington. My research interests lie in statistical machine learning, optimization and reinforcement learning. At school, I worked with Dr. Martha White, mainly on representation learning with regularized Dictionary Learning.
